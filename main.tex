\subsection{Steps for purchase request application:}
   \centering 
    \hlt{\textbf{KEEP RECEIPT FROM COMPANY AND SIGN-IN SHEET!}}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Proceed to make an online order from the allowed list of places to buy stuff from
    \item Go to checkout and take a screenshot to upload at the end of application
    \item Make sure the amount in the screenshot is the same as the input "Requested Amount"
    \item "Payment Method" choose "University Requisition/Purchase Order"
    \item For ideas under "Benefits to FIU", you can look at previous purchase requests
    \item Once purchase request is accepted, then you must go to RSOC finance office in GC and place the order via the phone , then transfer the call to RSOC accountant (Maybe: \textbf{Claudia Kettel}) with credit card for payment on food orders. (like pizza!)
    \item For orders from Publix, you must first pick up a voucher/money order to pay for items from publix.
    \item Email a copy of the receipt and sign-in sheet to RSOC accountant, for example; Claudia Kettel (ckettel@fiu.edu)
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Important Tips}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Purchase Request must be made \hlt{15 days} in advance of an event
    \item An event must be posted on panther connect and the flyer/poster must be uploaded with purchase request (Take a screenshot)
    \item Do not forget voucher/money order to cater from Publix, you can get it a day before the event.
    \item Someone in the RSOC finance office can help you when you go to place an order or pick up a voucher, \hlt{this is after the purchase request is accepted}
    \item Companies from which to place purchase requests are listed in the RSOC manual \cite{rsocManual}, for example, the Publix or Papa John’s Pizza on Flagler.
\end{itemize}
